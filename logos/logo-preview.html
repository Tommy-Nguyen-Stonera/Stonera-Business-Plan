Stonera Logo Comparison

Gold (current) vs Green (proposed) brand direction for the diamond icon and STONERA wordmark.

Why consider green? The gold version matches the current Stonera brand. The green version differentiates from competitors. No other Sydney stone fabricator uses green. Every major competitor (Avant Stone, WK Marble, SNB Stone, Universal Granite) uses gold or warm neutral tones. Green positions Stonera as immediately distinct in the market.
