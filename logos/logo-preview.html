Stonera Logo Concepts

Stone fabrication in Sydney since 2001. Five directions for the visual identity.

Concept A: Modified O Wordmark

Clean serif wordmark where the letter O features a thin horizontal line through its center, evoking a stone slab cross-section. A decorative underline bar anchors the composition.

Concept B: Stacked Slabs Icon + Text

Three thin horizontal slabs stacked with slight rightward offsets sit beside the wordmark, referencing stone slabs leaning on a fabrication rack.

Concept C: Mitre Cut S Avatar Mark

A bold custom S with 45-degree mitre-cut terminals, set inside a circle. Designed to work as a social media avatar and brand mark at any size.

Concept D: Stone Edge Wordmark

Every letter stroke terminates with flat 45-degree angular cuts instead of traditional serifs, giving the typography a stone-cut character. Charcoal text with a veridian green accent line.

Concept E: Combined Brand Lockup

The Mitre Cut S mark paired with the full wordmark and tagline. This is the primary brand lockup for website headers, documents, and signage.
